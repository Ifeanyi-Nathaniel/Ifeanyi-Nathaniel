## Tactical Overview

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineer working at the crossroads of **next-generation wireless communications, integrated sensing, and signal processing**.

My research focuses on the **design and simulation of OFDM-based ISAC systems that jointly optimize radar sensing and communication performance within standard-compliant 5G NR frameworks**.

### Research Interests

- Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) for 5G/6G networks
- OFDM waveform design for dual-functional radar-communication systems
- Physical layer resource allocation and pilot density optimization
- Simulation-based performance analysis of next-generation wireless systems
- Intelligent resource scheduling for heterogeneous wireless networks
